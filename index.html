Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shear Beauty a salon?

No. Shear Beauty is a private suite/chair rental business for independent beauty professionals.

Do you provide clients or walk-in traffic?

No. Beauty professionals are responsible for building and managing their own clientele.

What is included with the suite?

Your suite includes a professional chair, floor mat, and wall-mounted mirror. The building includes shared amenities like WiFi, laundry, shampoo bowls, coffee bar, parking, and 24/7 access.

How much is rent?

Weekly rent is $210. Payments can be handled online through the tenant portal once setup is complete.

Is financing available?

Suite financing may be available. Ask management for details. Terms and conditions apply.

Can tenants sell products or merch?

Product and merch sales may be available through Square. Online Shear Beauty merch sales belong to Shear Beauty. In-person assisted sales may qualify for a tracked referral credit if approved by management.
